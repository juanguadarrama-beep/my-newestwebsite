Fresh Fettuccine Alfredo

Ingredients

  • 10–12 oz fettuccine
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1–1½ cups grated Parmesan
  • Salt + black pepper

Steps

  1. Boil salted water; cook pasta. Save 1 cup pasta water and drain.
  2. Melt butter on low; add cream and warm gently.
  3. Stir in Parmesan until smooth.
  4. Toss pasta in sauce; loosen with pasta water if needed.
  5. Season and serve.
